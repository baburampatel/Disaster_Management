📝 Reporting Guidelines

  • Ensure your personal safety before reporting
  • Provide as accurate a location as possible (village, landmark, GPS if available)
  • Estimate the number of people affected
  • Describe visible damage and injuries
  • Mention any ongoing hazards (fire, rising water, structural damage)
  • Your report will be reviewed by administrators and forwarded to relevant authorities
